What is BMI?

BMI (Body Mass Index) is a measure of body fat based on your weight in relation to your height. It is widely used to determine if a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Categories:

  • Underweight: BMI < 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5 – 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25 – 29.9
  • Obesity: BMI ≥ 30
